My Projects

Space, Weather, Distance, Flask

The task is to build a Flask website using Python, featuring live data about the ISS, like its coordinates and image. It also shows weather below the ISS in Celsius using an API, identify the country underneath through reverse geolocation, display country details and flags as needed, compute distance from a set location, and guarantee data updates when the page is reloaded. Multiple APIs are used for data retrieval, with efficiency in mind.

Environmental Assessment Project on GIS Mapping

The project requires creating a web presentation using ArcGIS Online. Working for GeoEnv Ltd, the task involves lake water sampling and tree health assessment across six lakes in Greater Sudbury. Using ArcGIS Online, a Web Map and a Story Map Journal are created to showcase the data. The Story Map Journal explains lake sampling, tree assessment, watershed importance, and barren rings reclamation efforts.

Infographic: Low Income Among Persons With A Disability in Canada

This infographic, based on Statistics Canada's report "Low Income Among Persons With A Disability in Canada," explores vital data from the study's overview. It shows the age distribution of low-income individuals with disabilities using a bar graph, shedding light on age groups most affected by low income and the relationship between educational attainment and low income among individuals with disabilities, unveiling patterns of vulnerability across education levels. Through these visual representations, the infographic provides insights into the multifaceted aspects of low income within Canada's disabled population.

Infographic: The Proportion of Personnel Engagement

This infographic draws from Statistics Canada's "Personnel Engaged in Research and Development" data, presenting a snapshot of R&D personnel across sectors and occupational categories. It showcases the number of personnel engaged in R&D within sectors such as Federal Government, Provincial Government, Business Enterprise, Higher Education, and Higher Nonprofit. Moreover, the infographic delves into the distribution of occupational categories—Researchers, Technicians, and Support Staff—providing insights into their proportions within the R&D workforce. Through this visualization, the infographic highlights the diversity and distribution of R&D personnel, shedding light on the roles they play across sectors and occupational categories.

GIS Mapping: US Mass Shootings 1982 to 2019

This mapping creates an ArcGIS Online web app to display US mass shootings (1982-2019). Craft a map, customize layers, and save it. Build a web app from the map, customize themes and tools, enable search, and save. Tasks involve focusing on states, generating named PDF maps, measuring distances, and identifying dates. Final evaluation covers design, tools, and functionality.

GIS Mapping: Baltimore Fast Food Chains

This mapping creates a Web Map and Web App using ArcGIS Online to showcase fast food chain restaurants in Baltimore, Maryland. After signing in and saving the map, customize layers with attributes and symbols. Conduct a 'Find Hot Spots' analysis, determining a maximum of 10 points. Create a Web App using Web App Builder with a chosen theme and layout, adding widgets for Legend, Measurement, and Basemap Gallery. Save the Web App, concluding the process.

GIS Mapping: Houston Evacuation Map

This mapping shares an evacuation map for Houston, Texas, in preparation for a hurricane. Tasks include following instructions to add layers, navigate the map, change styles, add demographic data, save and share the map, create a web app, configure it, and edit item details.
